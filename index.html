About Me

A Voice for Change

I am Nitin Jagannath Gagre — a passionate public speaker, social change advocate, and NGO professional from Pune, Maharashtra. My journey in public speaking has taken me to stages across Maharashtra, where I have spoken on some of the most pressing issues of our time.

With an academic foundation in Biotechnology and Sociology, I bring both scientific thinking and deep social awareness to every conversation. I believe that words, delivered with conviction, have the power to change communities.

My mission is simple: to use my voice to advocate for environmental conservation, child rights, gender equality, and rural development — one seminar, one speech, one person at a time.

🗣 Marathi (Native) 🗣 Hindi (Fluent) 🗣 English (Proficient)
